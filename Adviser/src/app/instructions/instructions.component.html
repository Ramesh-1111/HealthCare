Instructions

monitor_weight

Height Measurement:

  1. Stand Straight: Keep your posture upright and face forward.
  2. Position Correctly: Stand at the marked spot under the sensor.
  3. Stay Still: Do not move while the robot measures your height.
  4. Remove Obstructions: Take off hats, caps, or anything on your head.

Weight Measurement:

  1. Stand Straight: Keep your posture upright and balanced.
  2. Position Correctly: Stand at the center of the weighing platform.
  3. Stay Still: Avoid moving or shifting weight while the measurement is taken.
  4. Remove Extra Items: Take off heavy accessories like bags, coats, or shoes.

Temperature Measurement

  1. Keep your wrist or back of your hand facing the sensor.
  2. Show your hand 1 cm away from the sensor
  3. Avoid moving while the sensor takes the reading.

Pulse Oximeter Measurement:

  1. Rest your hand on a flat surface and keep it still.
  2. Insert your index or middle finger fully into the oximeter.
  3. Stay still during the measurement to get an accurate reading.
  4. Ensure your fingernail is clean and free of polish or artificial nails for accurate results.
